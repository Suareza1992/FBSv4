# FitBySuarez — AI Marketing Prompts

A collection of ready-to-use AI prompts for marketing the FitBySuarez platform across social media, content, sales outreach, visual assets, and ongoing communications. Each prompt is brand-aware, Spanish-market-focused, and written to reflect the premium gold-on-black aesthetic and PR fitness culture.

**Brand quick reference:**
- Platform: FitBySuarez — fitbysuarez.com
- Colors: Gold (#FFDB89) on black
- Primary language: Spanish (for client-facing copy)
- Unique angle: Professional trainer management platform replacing WhatsApp + spreadsheets
- Payment methods: ATH Móvil, Venmo, PayPal
- No app download required
